About the archive


The recipes found here have been collected and used by my family on my mother's side for over 50 years. The bulk of the recipes here were collected by my mum in the late 1990s to early 2000s, with many family favourites coming from issues of Canadian Living magazine. The rest of the recipes come from places like the cooking classes my late grandparents took in the early 1970s, newspapers, product boxes, calendars, email exchanges with family friends, and other personal kitchen experiments.

  Despite my best efforts to reorganize and preserve the handful of binders that contain all these recipes, eventually there will come a time when the original copies fall victim to the years of grease stains and love put into them. And so, this digital archive was born; my goal is to eventually digitize all these recipes so they can live on for future generations to enjoy.


- Ava
