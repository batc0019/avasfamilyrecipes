Brownie Pudding


story

This is another long-time family favourite recipe gifted to my mum by a family friend. As you can maybe tell by the splatters and faded page, this brownie pudding ("Chocolate Fudge Brownie" or "CFB" as it's affectionately been dubbed) has been made over and over again and remains a household staple for dessert.


ingredients

Ingredients list
1 cup flour
1/2 cup sugar
1/4 cup cocoa
1 tsp baking powder
3/4 tsp salt
1/4 cup nuts (optional)
1/2 cup milk
2 tbsp tsp melted butter
1 tsp vanilla
1/2 cup brown sugar, packed
1/4 cup cocoa
1 1/3 cup hot water

tools
  • bowls
  • whisk
  • large spoon
  • 8" square baking dish
  • kettle

instructions
  1. Mix flour, sugar, cocoa, baking powder and salt together. Stir in nuts.
  2. Add milk, melted butter and vanilla. Mix well. Spread batter in greased 8" baking dish.
  3. Combine brown sugar and cocoa powder. Sprinkle evenly over batter. Pour hot water carefully over batter. Do not stir.
  4. Bake at 350 F for 40-45 minutes. Serve warm with ice cream!! :)

Note: mix brown sugar, cocoa and water together instead. Pour over batter, but make sure not to make a hole.
